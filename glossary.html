Acceptable Losses:

The amount of students that are allowed to be lost when the findAvail.py script finds new times for the section.

Banner ID:

A number representing a specific student.

checkMove.py:

The script that checks if a certain timeslot will work for a section and lists any conflicts with the proposed timeslot.

Class Code:

A set of two chracters that represents a student's classification. Examples would be 'SR' for seniors and 'JR' for juniors.

CRN:

A number representing a specific section of a course. Only unique for a single term.

Enrollment:

A student attending a section of course, their grade, and the term for which the section took place.

findAvail.py:

The script that lists out all of the possible times a section can be moved to given a section and acceptable student losses in the move.

Instructor:

The person that teaches a section of a course. Exists as part of the section table.

Instructor ID:

A number representing a specific instructor. Exists as part of the sections table.

Section:

A specific instance of a course.

Student:

Someone that takes enrolls in sections to receive a grade.

Term Code:

A number representing a specific semester in a year.

Timeslot:

The days and times that a section meets. Usually MWF or TR, but not always.
